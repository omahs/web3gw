# Transfer Eth TFT Action

> transfers tft from an account on ethereum to another

- action name: !!eth.transfer.tft
- parameters:
  - destination [required]
  - amount [required]

## Example

```md
  !!eth.transfer.tft
      destination: b27a31f1b0af2946b7f582768f03239b1ec07c2c
      amount: 100
```
